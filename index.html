Start Your Engines!

The game is simple! First things first you will need to enter your name. Then you can begin typing in words!
Typing words will give you points if they match our dictionary of the 1000 most common english words. The longer the word the better!
You only have 5 attempts so try to think hard!

Scoring

The game scores you based on the length of the words you use. Every valid word gives 50 points and every word longer than 3 characters adds an additional 25 points!
